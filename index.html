SecureScan AI

Privacy Policy

SecureScan AI is built to help users detect and redact sensitive information from images and PDF documents with privacy-first, on-device workflows.

Last updated: April 2026

Local Processing

SecureScan AI processes OCR, sensitive data detection, redaction previews, and export generation directly on your device. Your documents are not uploaded to a remote server for AI processing.

Stored Data

Imported files, exported redacted copies, manual redaction boxes, app settings, and local usage state are stored inside the app sandbox on your device.

Biometric and Passcode Access

Face ID, Touch ID, and the optional app passcode are used only to unlock the app locally. Biometric data remains managed by Apple’s Secure Enclave and is not accessible to SecureScan AI.

Sharing

Documents are shared only when you explicitly use the iOS share sheet. SecureScan AI does not silently transmit files, extracted text, or OCR results.

Delete Your Data

You can remove imported files, exported copies, settings, and cached local usage data at any time from the in-app setting Clear All Local Data.

In-App Purchases

SecureScan AI may offer optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. SecureScan AI does not receive or store your payment card details.
